-MORE SOLUTIONS-


1. Trim regularly

Trimming removes split ends and other damaged hair, which can travel up the hair shaft and cause breakage. Trim your hair every six to eight weeks. Trimming your hair can have many benefits, including: Preventing split ends: Trimming removes damaged ends that can cause breakage and fraying. Promoting healthy hair growth: Trimming stimulates hair follicles and encourages new growth. Improving hair texture: Trimming can make hair smoother, shinier, and less prone to tangles and frizz. Detangling: Freshly cut ends can help to de-tangle your hair. Thickness: Trimming can give your hair an instant boost of thickness by trimming those fried and thinned out ends. Making styling easier: Trimming eliminates rough ends, which can make styling much easier. Making your hair look thicker and healthier.

2. Drink water

Staying hydrated helps nourish and hydrate your hair, preventing brittle hair and split ends.Drinking water has many benefits for hair, including: Hydration Water helps keep your hair and scalp hydrated, which can prevent hair from becoming dry, dull, or brittle. Hair growth Water supports vitamins that contribute to hair growth, and it helps energize hair follicles. Scalp health Drinking water can help prevent dandruff, itchiness, and other scalp issues. Hair strength Drinking enough water can help strengthen hair, which can lead to increased growth. Reduced stress Dehydration can increase stress hormones, which can lead to hair loss. Shiny hair Drinking warm water can stimulate nerve endings in the hair roots, which may lead to shiny, soft hair.

3. Eat a balanced diet:

Your hair's health starts from within, so a balanced diet is important.

A balanced diet with enough protein, vitamins, and minerals is important for healthy hair growth. Some foods that can help with hair growth include:Eggs,Avocado,Spinach,Nuts and seeds,Oysters,Sweet potatoes,Greek yogurt,Salmon, mackerel, and tuna.

4. Be gentle with your hair

Hair is delicate when it’s wet, so use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush to detangle wet hair. If you have thick, curly hair, the best time to comb is in the shower before rinsing out your conditioner. If you have straight hair, let your hair dry a bit before combing. Slowly comb the ends of your hair first then keep combing higher to detangle your hair with minimal damage.

5.Protect your hair from sun

Protect your hair from environmental damage caused by the sun, wind, and pollution by wearing hats or scarves, and using UV-protectant hair products.

6. Surya Namaskar

- Traditionally, the practice of Surya Namaskar was used as a means of paying respect to the sun. In Indian culture from which the practice came, the sun is regarded as the source of all life, and it is therefore of great importance.

- In Hinduism, Surya is the God of the sun, understood to be the creator of the universe, and in Vedic tradition the sun is symbolic of consciousness and the Divine. As such, Surya Namaskar is considered to be one of the most important yoga practices.

- Surya Namaskar is also referred to as Sun Salutation in English.

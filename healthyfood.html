- Good Food for a Healthy Hair -


1.Protein-rich foods

- Eggs: One extra-large egg contains about seven grams of protein

-Chicken and turkey: Lean poultry that's high in protein

-Fish: A high-quality protein source that also contains omega-3 fatty acids and vitamin B-12

-Paneer and soybeans: Good sources of protein for vegetarians

-Greek yogurt: Contains protein, B vitamins, selenium, and vitamin D

2.Nuts and seeds

-Avocados: Contain vitamin E, potassium, vitamin B, and folic acid

-Sweet potatoes: Contain beta-carotene, a form of vitamin A that can help prevent hair loss

-Cinnamon: Contains polyphenols, which have antimicrobial properties that protect the scalp from infections

-Berries: Contain antioxidants that protect cells from damage and are also key for collagen production

-Carrots: Contain high levels of vitamin A, which can make hair thicker, stronger, and shinier

-Dark green vegetables: Contain vitamins A and C, which are needed to produce sebum
